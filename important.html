Details: Law enforcement officials are seeking information about certain images which may help lead to the identification and rescue of child victims. The FBI utilizes image analysis to identify clues to where a child may be held. By recognizing these clues and/or sharing additional observations from the backgrounds of these images, you may help first to identify and rescue the child victim(s) and second to capture the perpetrator. There is no clue or piece of information too small.
